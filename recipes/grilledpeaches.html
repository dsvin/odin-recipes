Grilled Peaches and Cream

Description

Easy desert to make for that smokey grilled flavor combined with the sweetness

Ingredients

  • 4 peaches, halved and pitted
  • 2 tablespoons clover honey
  • 1 cup soft cream cheese with honey and nuts
  • 1 tablespoon vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Preheat a grill for medium-high heat
  2. Brush peaches with a light coating of oil.
  3. Grill for 5 minutes, or until the surfaces have nice grill marks. Turn the peaches over, and drizzle with a bit of honey. Place a dollop of the cream cheese spread in the place where the pit was.
  4. Grill for 2 to 3 more minutes, or until the filling is warm. Serve immediately.
